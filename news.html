Participation in Bapco Gas Health, Safety, and Environment Exhibition

Nasser Scientific and Technical Center participated in the Bapco Gas Health, Safety, and Environment Exhibition, which took place at the company’s premises under the theme “Health, Safety, and Environment Week.” Students presented their innovative project on a gas detection system for enclosed spaces using Arduino technology. This system is designed to assist workers in high-risk environments in early detection of gas leaks, contributing to safety and security in the workplace.

Enhancing Mechanical Engineering Knowledge

The visit of mechanical engineering students to Nissan aims to enhance their practical understanding of engineering applications in the automotive industry. Through direct exposure to the latest technologies and innovations used in car manufacturing and maintenance, this visit provides a valuable opportunity for students to gain new experiences and strengthen their academic knowledge by linking theory with practice in an industrial setting.

Nasser Scientific Center Hosts Five’s Service Gulf Delegation

The training arm of Nasser Scientific and Technical Center welcomed a delegation from Five’s Service Gulf to explore the training programs offered. This collaboration, which resulted in the successful implementation of the ‘Work-Integrated Training’ program for students at the center.
